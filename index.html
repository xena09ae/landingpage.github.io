How It Works

Simple SMS-based workflow for crisis situations

1

Registration

Field workers register displaced individuals via SMS with format:

REG [Name]*[Age]*[Father's Phone]*[LAT]*[LONG]

System responds with unique ID for future reference.

2

Location Updates

Individuals update their location when moving:

UPDATE#[ID]*[LAT]*[LONG]

Coordinates are stored and shared with authorized NGOs.

3

Emergency Alerts

Critical situations are reported immediately:

SOS#[ID]*[Emergency Type]

Automatically includes last known location.

4

Family Search

Locate family members through verified contacts:

FIND#[Phone Number]

Returns last known location if authorized.
